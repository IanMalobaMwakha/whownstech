ABOUT

In brief, Ian is a 20-year-old male living in Nairobi, Kenya with a strong passion for coding, watching movies, football and traveling. I have watched a wide range of movies, with the SciFi "The Orville" being one of my favorites. Programming has been an interest of mine for a few years now, I have a good understanding and hands-on experience with few programming languages i.e. Python, Java and Javascript and some frameworks-MERN Stack for full stack web development. Traveling is another passion of mine, and I have visited various places around the world, each offering a unique experience. With a strong interest in technology, I passionately write articles during my free time with a focus of providing insightful analysis and thought-provoking commentary.
